Our Story

Craftsmanship
passed down through
generations.

Founded in the heart of Kathmandu, Sonam Rugs began with a small group of master weavers and a single belief: that quality should never be compromised. Today, our rugs adorn the living rooms, hallways, and studies of discerning homes around the world — from New York penthouses to London townhouses and Parisian apartments.

Each carpet is woven entirely by hand using traditional Tibetan knotting techniques, with natural Himalayan wool and pure silk sourced directly from trusted suppliers. No shortcuts. No machines.

How it's made

Six steps from
fibre to floor.

01

Designing

Each pattern begins as a hand-drawn cartoon on graph paper, every knot plotted by our master designers.

02

Dyeing

Natural fibres are dyed in small batches using traditional mineral and botanical dye recipes refined over centuries.

03

Weaving

Artisans hand-knot each row of the carpet on vertical looms, a process that can take months for larger pieces.

04

Washing

Freshly cut rugs are washed with mountain water to soften fibres and reveal the full depth of their colour.

05

Stretching

Rugs are stretched onto frames and dried under the Kathmandu sun, ensuring they lay perfectly flat for life.

06

Packing

Each finished carpet is inspected, rolled, and packed with archival materials for safe delivery worldwide.

How long does a custom rug take to make?
+

Timelines depend on size and complexity. A standard 8 × 10 ft rug typically takes 10–16 weeks. Larger or more intricate pieces may take up to 6 months. We keep you updated throughout the process with photos from the workshop.

Can I order a completely custom design?
+

Absolutely. We welcome fully bespoke commissions. Share your space dimensions, design inspiration, and preferred colour palette and our team will create a digital mockup before any weaving begins.

What materials do you use?
+

We use hand-spun Himalayan highland wool and natural silk sourced from trusted suppliers in Nepal and Tibet. All dyes are plant- or mineral-based. We do not use synthetic fibres or chemical dyes in any of our carpets.

Do you ship internationally?
+

Yes — we ship to over 60 countries worldwide. All shipments are fully insured, carefully packed with archival materials, and come with a certificate of authenticity and care guide.

How do I care for my Sonam rug?
+

Vacuum regularly with a low suction setting. Rotate the rug every 1–2 years to ensure even wear. For deep cleaning, we recommend professional rug washing every 3–5 years. Avoid direct sunlight and damp conditions.
